Why Cruise Passengers Choose This Tour

A St Kitts private tour puts your group in control. Instead of following a fixed shared itinerary, you work with a driver-guide to prioritise Brimstone Hill, Timothy Hill, Cockleshell Beach, Romney Manor, rainforest trails or Basseterre — at a pace that suits everyone. Private tours are especially popular with families, photographers and multi-generational groups.

What to Expect

  • Private pickup at Port Zante in a dedicated vehicle (van or SUV)
  • Pre-discussed itinerary based on your interests and ship schedule
  • Flexible stop durations — linger at viewpoints or beaches as you choose
  • Local commentary from your driver-guide throughout the day
  • Return to ship with agreed buffer before all-aboard time

Good to Know Before Booking

  • Share your ship name, arrival time and all-aboard deadline upfront
  • Private tours typically priced per vehicle or per group — compare quotes
  • Railway segments may require separate tickets on some private itineraries
  • Always check your cruise ship arrival and departure times before booking

What to Bring

  • Comfortable shoes suitable for your chosen stops
  • Swimwear and towel if beach time is included
  • Sun protection and water
  • List of must-see priorities to share with your guide

Best For

  • Families with children or older relatives
  • Groups of 4–8 seeking value and flexibility
  • Photographers wanting extended time at viewpoints
  • Repeat visitors combining favourite spots in one day

Who Should Choose a Different Tour

  • Solo travellers or couples on a tight budget — shared tours cost less
  • Passengers wanting the full railway experience — book a dedicated railway tour
  • Water-focused days — a catamaran cruise is structured for snorkelling
  • Those happy with standard routes — an island highlights tour covers essentials efficiently
