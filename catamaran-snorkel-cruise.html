St Kitts Catamaran Snorkel Cruise

Compare St Kitts catamaran excursions — sail the coastline, snorkel Caribbean reefs and enjoy a relaxed day on the water.

Cruise Passenger Snapshot

Duration 4–4.5 hours
Activity Level Easy–Moderate
Type Shared group
Port Pickup Port Zante / beach club
Scenic Value Very Good
Return Confidence Medium–High

Why Cruise Passengers Choose This Tour

St Kitts catamaran excursions combine sailing along the island's scenic coastline with snorkelling in sheltered coves. Cruise passengers choose catamaran tours for time on the water, tropical fish and coral viewing, and a social atmosphere with drinks and music on deck — a classic Caribbean port-day experience.

What to Expect

  • Transfer from port to catamaran departure point (often Frigate Bay area)
  • Sailing along St Kitts' coast with commentary and refreshments
  • Snorkel stop at a reef or sheltered bay with equipment provided
  • Time to swim, sunbathe on deck or relax in a cove
  • Return to port — some tours include lunch or open bar

Good to Know Before Booking

  • Tours may be rescheduled or modified in rough sea conditions
  • Basic swimming ability recommended for snorkelling segments
  • Confirm whether lunch, drinks and snorkel gear are included
  • Always check your cruise ship arrival and departure times before booking

What to Bring

  • Swimwear, towel and change of clothes
  • Reef-safe sunscreen and rash guard
  • Waterproof phone case or dry bag
  • Motion sickness remedy if you are prone to seasickness

Best For

  • Snorkellers and water lovers
  • Couples and groups wanting a fun, social port day
  • Passengers who have already seen St Kitts' land sights
  • Confident swimmers comfortable in open water

Who Should Choose a Different Tour

  • Non-swimmers or those uncomfortable on boats
  • History enthusiasts — try Brimstone Hill
  • Land-based scenery fans — the Scenic Railway stays ashore
  • Short port calls under 6 hours — timing may be tight
