Beach & Sightseeing Tour

Combine scenic St Kitts sightseeing with relaxed beach time — the ideal shore excursion for sun seekers visiting on a cruise.

Cruise Passenger Snapshot

Duration 4–5 hours
Activity Level Easy
Type Shared group
Port Pickup Port Zante
Scenic Value Very Good
Return Confidence High

Why Cruise Passengers Choose This Tour

Beach and sightseeing combo tours balance culture and relaxation in one port day. Cruise passengers get a scenic drive — often including Timothy Hill or Basseterre highlights — plus extended beach time at Frigate Bay, South Friars or Cockleshell. It is one of the most popular formats for travellers who want both photos and swimming without booking separate excursions.

What to Expect

  • Coach pickup at Port Zante with guided commentary
  • Scenic drive with photo stops at key viewpoints
  • 1.5–2 hours at a selected beach (Frigate Bay or peninsula beaches)
  • Free time to swim, relax or visit beach bars
  • Return to cruise terminal before departure

Good to Know Before Booking

  • Beach destination varies by operator — confirm which beach is included
  • Beach chair and umbrella rentals usually cost extra
  • Some tours include a welcome drink; lunch is rarely included
  • Always check your cruise ship arrival and departure times before booking

What to Bring

  • Swimwear, towel and reef-safe sunscreen
  • Cash for beach rentals, food and drinks
  • Cover-up for the coach ride
  • Waterproof bag for electronics

Best For

  • Sun seekers who still want some island sightseeing
  • Families wanting easy, low-effort beach access from the port
  • First-time visitors who prioritise sand and sea
  • Passengers on warm-weather itineraries seeking a classic Caribbean beach day

Who Should Choose a Different Tour

  • History enthusiasts wanting deep fortress time — try Brimstone Hill
  • Peninsula photography focus — the dedicated peninsula tour covers more coastal stops
  • Snorkellers — a catamaran cruise offers more time in the water
  • Railway fans — book the Scenic Railway
