Why St Kitts

One of the Caribbean's Most Scenic Cruise Destinations

St Kitts combines scenic rail journeys, colonial history, panoramic viewpoints, beaches, catamaran cruises and rainforest experiences in a single port day. Cruise passengers arriving at Port Zante in Basseterre can explore a UNESCO-listed fortress, ride a historic narrow-gauge railway, snorkel from a catamaran, or drive the dramatic south east peninsula — all within a few hours of the ship.

Whether you are searching for St Kitts cruise excursions, St Kitts cruise port tours, or a tailored St Kitts private tour, this guide helps you match the right experience to your schedule, mobility and interests.

Signature Experience

St Kitts Railway Tour

The St Kitts railway tour is among the most distinctive shore excursions in the Eastern Caribbean. Originally built to transport sugar cane, the narrow-gauge line now carries passengers on open-sided carriages through lush valleys, past old plantations and along dramatic coastal stretches.

  • Ideal for cruise passengers who prefer a seated, scenic experience
  • Usually combined with a short sightseeing coach segment
  • Popular on ships with limited port time — book early

UNESCO Heritage

Brimstone Hill Fortress Tours

One of the best-preserved colonial fortifications in the Caribbean, Brimstone Hill Fortress is a must-see for history lovers. Perched 800 feet above the sea, the citadel offers panoramic views and a fascinating glimpse into St Kitts' colonial past.

  • Included on many St Kitts island tours and shore excursions
  • Moderate walking on uneven stone surfaces
  • Combine with Basseterre or beach stops on full-day itineraries

St Kitts Island Tours & Highlights
